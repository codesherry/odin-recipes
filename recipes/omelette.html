Omelette Recipe

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Eggs.
  • 1 Medium Size Onion.
  • 1 Medium Size Green Chilli.
  • Butter/Oil of your choice.

Steps:

  1. Chop the Onion and Green Chilli.
  2. Put them into a bowl.
  3. Now, add the Eggs into it.
  4. Add some Salt, Red Paprika Powder and Tumeric Powder.
  5. Mix everything well.
  6. Now, take a pan and put the butter/oil of your choice.
  7. When the pan is hot, put the omelette mixture into it.
  8. Cook both sides equally.
  9. Sheesh, have it with a coffee and you are good to go for the day!
