Boiled Rice with Boneless Chicken Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Steps
    • Chicken Boneless Curry

Description:

This dish is a combination of 2 dishes Boiled Rice (I know it's not a proper dish) and Chicken Boneless Curry. This is very easy to make and very delicious to eat. I always eat it as my post-workout meal but often eat it in lunch or dinner. Your can make as many platers as once as you like and microwave them before you want to eat.However, this is the recipe for 1 plater, I usually make 2 platers at once and store it for next day for my ease.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Rice.
  • 150g of Boneless Chicken.
  • ¼ cup of Vegetable oil.
  • 2 Medium Size Tomatoes.
  • 1 tbsp of Ginger and Garlic Paste.
  • Salt and Spices (Red Paprika Powder, Tumeric Powder, Whole Coriander Powder).
  • For Garnish (Julien Ginger, Coriander, Green Chillies, ½ tbsp Cumin Powder).

Steps:

Chicken Boneless Curry:

  1. Put ¼ cup of Vegetable Oil into a wok-like pot.
  2. Cut your Chicken Boneless into little Pieces and shift them into the pot.
  3. Fry the Chicken Boneless Pieces for 10-12 minutes (on Medium flame).
  4. During that time, cut down the Tomatoes and put them into a juicer, beat it for 1 minute and then add 1 tbsp of Ginger and Garlic Paste, Salt and Spices.
  5. Make sure that your Chicken is little bit golden brown, then pour the mixture into the pot.
  6. Stir, and give it a lid for another 10-12 minutes (Stir every 2 minutes to make sure the it doesn't stick to the surface).
  7. During this time, Julien the Ginger and cut some Coriander and Green Chillies.
  8. Now, remove the lid and wait until all the excess liquid is gone.
  9. Now, Garnish it and add ½ tbsp Cumin Powder.
  10. Wallah, This gonna be the best Chicken your have ever eaten!

Boiled Rice:

  1. Wash 1 cup of rice.
  2. Pour 2 cups of water in a pot and boil it.
  3. When the water is totally boiled, add the washed rice into it.
  4. When all the water is soaked by the rice (with very little water left), give it a lid for 20 minutes (on low flame).
  5. After 20 minutes, remove the lid and say Wallah.

Now, move the rice to a plate and put the Chicken Boneless Curry on top along with the salad of your preference.
