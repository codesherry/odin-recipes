Oatmeal Recipe

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description:

Oatmeal is easy to make along with being healthy to eat. It is filled with complex carbohydrates. I normally eat it in my pre-workout meal and sometimes in the breakfast too.

Ingredients:

  • 40g Oats.
  • 1 tbsp Peanut Butter.
  • 3 Dates (for sweetness).
  • 2 Bananas.

Steps:

  1. Boil 1 cup of Water in a pot.
  2. Add 40g of Oats into a bowl.
  3. Put Â¾ of boiling water into the same bowl.
  4. Stir well.
  5. Put 1 tbsp of Peanut Butter into it.
  6. Stir again.
  7. Cut 2-3 Dates into little pieces and add it into the oatmeal.
  8. Stir again.
  9. Cut 2 Bananas and move to the bowl as the topping.
  10. Boom! Your Oatmeal is ready to eat.
Nutritional Values
Calories 494 kcal
Protein 14 g
Carbohydrates 107 g
