Oatmeal Recipe

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description:

Oatmeal is easy to make along with being healthy to eat. It is filled with complex carbohydrates. I normally eat it in my pre-workout meal and sometimes in the breakfast too.

Ingredients:

  • 40g Oats.
  • ¾ cup of Milk/Water.
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar/White Sugar (for sweetness).
  • Fruit of your choice.

Steps:

  1. Boil ¾ cup of Milk/Water in a pan.
  2. Add Brown Sugar/White Sugar into the boiling Milk/Water.
  3. Add 40g of Oats into it.
  4. Stir till you find your desired consistency.
  5. Now, Shift it into a bowl.
  6. Cut the Fruit of your choice into little pieces and add as the toping.
  7. NOTE: If your have Dry Fruits add those too as the topping along with some Geek Yogurt (if like so).
  8. Boom! Your Oatmeal is ready to eat.
Nutritional Values
Calories 494 kcal
Protein 14 g
Carbohydrates 107 g
